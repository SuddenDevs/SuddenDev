About

SUDDEN_DEV is a co-op survival game with a twist - you aren't the player! Write scripts to manipulate a character and work together to slay violent geometric primitives. Prove your genius by defending the gloriously circular core from the pointy anatomy of your enemies.

Play by scripting:

  • Scan the environment for enemies and powerups
  • Control your character's movement
  • Message other characters
  • Attack enemies

Clearing stages will require not only smart scripts, but novel team strategies. You won't survive on your own.
