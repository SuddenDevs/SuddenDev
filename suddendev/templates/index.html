About

SUDDEN_DEV is a co-op survival game with a twist; you don't play. Each player writes a script that controls their character. The aim of the game is to clear all the waves and protect the core.

Scripting lets you:

  • See everything near your character
  • Control your character's movements
  • Message other characters
  • Attack enemies that are in range

Clearing stages will require not only strong AI but clever team strategies. How effectively your characters play roles and communicate with each other will be vital.
